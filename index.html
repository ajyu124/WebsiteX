Let's get down to business.

Mission: Monta Vista FBLA’s mission is to bring together business, community and leadership through programs that emphasize achievement, growth, service, and progress while paralleling the classroom curriculum.

Short: Monta Vista FBLA is a Career & Technical Student Organization providing members opportunities to learn, explore, and experience business & leadership.

Monta Vista FBLA is a career-technical student organization dedicated to providing our members the opportunity to learn, explore, and experience business and leadership through our various competitions, projects, and networking opportunities. We are committed to empowering high school students and teaching them the skills necessary to succeed in the professional world.

Upcoming Conference

National Leadership ConferenceNLC

The National Leadership Conference (NLC) is the highest level of competition for FBLA members. Qualifiers from the State Leadership Conference have the opportunity to compete against other FBLA members from all over the country and across the globe. The National Leadership Conference is 5 days long, with various opportunities to attend workshops, participate in activities, network with others, and more. This year, the National Leadership Conference is being held in Anaheim, California, where members will have the opportunities to visit local attractions including Disneyland. We look forward to seeing you there!
